About Me

This is what I do.

Learn. Design. Inspire. Discover.

Hello, and thank you for visiting my website! My name is Georgia Riggs and I am a third-year student at Gonzaga University earning a B.A. in Psychology and minors in Digital Marketing and Leadership Studies. I've always had a passion for learning — learning about the world, myself, the humans that surround me. I seek to inspire others while continuing to allow myself to be inspired. I keep an open mind but hold firm to my core values. I listen intently and know when to speak up. I conduct myself in ways that make my loved ones proud.

I chose Gonzaga because of what it claimed to be — a school that values the whole person and a place that seeks to establish a welcoming environment for all. It hasn't let me down. I chose psychology and leadership studies because of my love for learning about others and how my desire to serve others. I chose digital marketing because it just. seemed. interesting. With knowledge about these three disciplines, I have the world at my fingertips. I truly believe I can accomplish anything. I just need to keep learning, designing, inspiring, and discovering.
